What are Bower Tabletop Tiles?

What are they?

Bower Tabletop Tiles are cut, painted, and etched pieces of foam that form your floors for your Dungeons, Taverns, Mansions, Crypts, and much more!

Grid

Bower Tabletop Tiles have marks that can be used to measure distance and show where your heroes and the monsters stand.

Multiple sizes

Whether you want to build a small room with a limited amount of space, or a larger battle scene, Bower Tabletop Tiles has got you covered. There are different sizes for you to choose from, so you can mix and match to make some great looking scenes!
